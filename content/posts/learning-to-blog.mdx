I’ve been seeing a lot of people online advertising their “vibed” solutions lately. 

What is a “vibed” solution? If you’re not in software - you probably aren’t aware of this, but newer LLM-based AI agents can create things fast. What used to take weeks can be done in a day. I’ll get more into the nuances of this in another post.

Ultimately, the point is that the barrier to entry is so low now. Things such as language learning apps, agent management dashboards, fitness companion apps - the classic kinds of developer projects.
